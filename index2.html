What is an Element?

Protons are particles with an electrical charge and when combined with neutrons that have no electrical charge they make up a nucleus, combining this nucleus with electrons, which are very small particles with a negative electrical energy make up an atom- the building blocks of the universe itself. A combination of two or more of the same atoms make up an Element. These Elements art displayed on the ‘periodic table’ arranged by atomic number, electron configuration, and recurring chemical properties.
