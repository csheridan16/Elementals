Sodium

Symbol: Na
Atomic number: 11
Atomic mass: 22.990
category: alkali metals
Sodium is a soft, silvery-white, highly reactive alkali metal being in group 1  of the periodic table, because it has a single electron in its outer shell. Sodium is the sixth most abundant element in the Earth's crust and exists in numerous minerals  such as feldspars, sodalite, and rock salt (NaCl). Sodium is a soft metal that tarnishes within seconds of being exposed to the air. It also reacts vigorously with water.
