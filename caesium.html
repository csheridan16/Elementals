Caesium

Symbol: Cs
Atomic number: 55
Atomic mass: 132.90545
category: alkali metals
Caesium is a soft, silvery-golden alkali metal with a melting point of 28.5 °C (83.3 °F), which makes it one of only five elemental  metals that are liquid at or near room temperature. Caesium has physical and chemical properties similar to those of rubidium and potassium. The most reactive of all metals and reacts with water even at −116 °C (−177 °F).
