Hydrogen

Symbol: H
Atomic number: 1
Atomic mass: 1.00784
category: Reactive nonmetal
Hydrogen with a standard atomic weight of 1.008, hydrogen is the lightest element in the periodic table. Hydrogen is the most abundant chemical substance in the Universe, constituting roughly 75% of all baryonic mass. Stars are mainly composed of hydrogen in the plasma state. The most common isotope of hydrogen, has one proton and no neutrons.
